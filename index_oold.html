Richard Oulahan Latin American Labor History Collection

This collection comes from Richard Oulahan, who worked for nearly four decades in Central America and the Andean Region as a Country Program and Regional Director for the American Institute of Free Labor Development (AIFLD), the Latin American and Caribbean assistance program of the AFL-CIO.

Explore collections on this page for historical information about Latin American labor and economic conditions as well as AIFLD operations. A sizable portion of material has been digitized and is available without restrictions.

Book and Pamphlet Lists

Titles added to Haverford Library from the collections of Ricard Oulahan and Dr. Jose Maria Mendez Jr. Materials are particularly rich for El Salvador and Honduras.
